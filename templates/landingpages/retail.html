Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about retail space for rent in Dubai

What is the average cost of retail space in Dubai? +
Retail rental prices range from AED 80 to AED 1,000+ per sq. ft depending on location and demand. Community retail spaces average AED 80–300 per sq. ft, high-street retail AED 300–800 per sq. ft, and premium mall retail starts at AED 1,000+ per sq. ft.
Is retail space in Dubai a good investment? +
Yes. Retail properties in Dubai offer high ROI (7–10% rental yields), strong tenant demand, tax-free rental income, and long-term capital appreciation — making them one of the most attractive commercial investments globally.
Which area is best for retail shops in Dubai? +
Popular areas include Downtown Dubai (luxury brands), Business Bay (startups and modern retail), Dubai Marina (restaurants and lifestyle), and Al Barsha (affordable high-demand spaces). Each area offers unique advantages based on target audience and business type.
Can foreigners rent retail properties in Dubai? +
Yes, foreigners can lease or invest in retail properties in designated areas across Dubai. Our team at KIF Realty can guide you through the process seamlessly.
What businesses are most profitable in Dubai retail spaces? +
Restaurants, fashion outlets, salons, and convenience stores consistently perform well. The growing F&B sector, experiential retail, and international brand expansion are among the strongest drivers of demand.
